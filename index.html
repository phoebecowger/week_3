Today my owner was away again, leaving me with her male companion. I hung out with him on his desk while he stared at a box for hours. Every once in a while he'd give me a pet. His pets are the best. Eventually the other kitten came around and stole all the attention from me. Ugh.

Hello readers, my name is Salem, or you can call me Sa-Sa. I’m turning 9 this year (I think).

I got adopted by a family at PetSmart, but when I got fleas, they threw me out! Their next door neighbor started giving me food, and eventually she let me in through her window and treated me, That was 7 years ago, and now I’m as plump as can be.

My favorite colors are black, pink, and white. My favorite pass time is watching birds and cuddling. I like wearing pajamas and sneaking a taste of whatever is in my owner’s cup!
